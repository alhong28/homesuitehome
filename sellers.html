Sellers

So you have decided to sell your property. Before you speak with a real estate professional, it’s crucial to understand why you’re selling your home and how quickly you’d like to sell. Having a clear motivation and timeline will allow us to understand your real estate goals and cater to your financial needs. Once you’ve done this, it’s time to...

Start Packing It may sound crazy to start packing months in advance of your move, but since you’ll eventually need to do this anyway, you might as well get organized now. I’m not suggesting that you pack up your kitchen and eat off paper plates, but you can sort through your storage closets, attic, basement or garage to determine what you want to keep, what to give away and what to sell. Boxing up items will make your space look larger and neater when it’s time to show your home.

Clear Away the Clutter If you’ve ever visited a home that has been professionally staged, you’ll never see a drawings loosely hanging on the refrigerator or a pile of unread mail on a table. Buyers need empty space so they can envision themselves living in your home. While you may not always want to pay a staging company for professionally selected decor, you’ll want to keep your home as neat as possible. This will help you sell your home in less time and at a higher price.

Improve Your Home It typically doesn’t pay off to do a major renovation project before you sell, but making minor repairs and improvements will make your home look fresher to buyers. Small fixes, such as patching up holes and dings on walls, replacing the caulk and grout in your bathroom, updating light fixtures, and changing doorknobs for a clean, uniform look. You can also never go wrong with painting rooms in shades of white or light grey.

Choose the right agent…US! There are passive seller’s agents who simply list your property on the MLS. And then there are agents who will use every resource at their disposal to make your property stand out from the crowd. Your choice of a listing agent will make a big difference in how quickly your home sells and how much of a profit you’ll realize. I provide my clients with an extensive marketing plan and in-depth market knowledge to help you make an informed decision. Don’t just go with someone who tells you they can sell for the highest price. Instead, choose someone who can present you with a detailed market analysis and implements proven methods!

Research Your Market Before putting your home on the market, you’ll want to know how much your property is worth. This is where my expertise can help. I can show you what similar homes in your area have recently sold for, and we can determine what pricing strategy to use: low, fair market, or high asking price. Each strategy has its advantages, and depending on your particular market you can expect to sell quicker or above your asking price by having an expert negotiate for you.
