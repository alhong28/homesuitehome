Max Tang

Born in Tapei, Tawain and raised in Edison, NJ. Max Tang is a NJ Realtor, motorsports enthusiast, and self taught fitness guru.

Meet The Man

There are two most important things when it comes to life: physical fitness and financial fitness. With a degree in accounting and a background in personal training, Max analyzes real estate from the perspective of an investor. His million dollar question: are the numbers healthy?

Born in the metro area in Taiwan and raised in the suburbs of NJ, Max was exposed to 2 sides to everything, especially the things most important to him: language, food, and culture.

Max graduated from Rutgers University in 2012 with a BS in accounting and a determination to use his skill set in an untraditional way to help others. After realizing that his work consistently pulled him into the world of real estate, he decided to step into the NJ arena in 2017 and set foot in Jersey City.

Max visualized the idea of making the process of buying your first large investment an easy process, and thus HomeSuiteHomes was born. With a strong background in accounting and a penchant for financial stability, he developed a self-help tool for homebuyers and investors alike: a comprehensive real estate calculator. This tool has helped countless residential and commercial buyers make the most important business decision in their lives. He hopes to help many more who are about to make that very same decision.
